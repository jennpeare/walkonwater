Walk on Water

Who we are

Walk on Water was established in May of 2011 by Brian Burdett, who brought Standup Paddleboarding to Roswell and the Chattahoochee River. WoW, in partnership with the City of Roswell Parks and Recreation, provides paddleboarding courses SUP 101, PaddleYoga, and PaddleFit. WoW's partnership with the Chattahoochee Nature Center focuses on the basics of stand-up paddleboarding.

Like us on Facebook and interact with fellow paddleboarders! Be sure to post comments and your latest paddleboarding adventure.

Come paddleboard down the Chattahoochee with us!

Roswell Parks & Recreation

Welcome to Roswell

From the hundreds of programs offered to residents to the City’s beautiful trail system, Roswell’s recreation and parks services are second to none in the State of Georgia. Roswell has 890.38 acres of parkland, or 10.5 acres per 1,000 residents, which exceeds the national recommendation of 7 acres per 1,000 residents. Roswell Recreation & Parks Department is nationally accredited and has been recognized an unprecedented eight times by the Georgia Parks & Recreation Association for having the best department in the state.

Read more about Recreation, Parks, Historic and Cultural Affairs at www.roswellgov.com.

Paddle101

Paddle101 focuses on the fundamentals of paddle and stroke techniques, board control, and navigation. The course will also go into the finer aspects of paddleboarding, including theory, self-launching, self-rescue, posture, balance, endurance, equipment advice, basic water rescue, critical decision making, and etiquette, with emphasis on safety and weather assessment. Paddle board, paddle and PFD will be provided.

Chattahoochee Nature Center

Explore the Chattahoochee

The Chattahoochee Nature Center is located on the Chattahoochee River in Roswell. It sits on a beautiful site comprised of 127 acres of native plants and gardens that showcase the beauty of Georgia. The CNC has a River Boardwalk, Discovery Center, wetland demonstration gardens and woodland trails that are home to over 50 species of injured, non-releasable wildlife. For 30 years, this facility has continued to grow and reach out to citizens as a place to explore new ideas and expand the awareness of the natural world.

Stand-up Paddleboarding School

Come out to the Chattahoochee Nature Center for a paddleboard class in a Georgia Wetlands pond. We help those new to paddleboarding (and those who just want to practice) learn the basics of stand-up paddleboarding. This class will teach how to enter and exit the water safely, as well as the correct paddling techniques. Come out and try this fun and exciting way to enjoy Chattahoochee Nature Center! This class is suited for all ages 8 and up. Paddle board, paddle and PFD will be provided.
