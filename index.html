Stand-up Paddleboarding SUP School

Now is your chance to pick-up a new way to experience the Chattahoochee. Brian Burdett with Walk on Water Adventures, LLC helps those new to paddleboarding (and those who just want to practice) learn the basics of stand-up paddleboarding. This class will teach entering and exiting the water safely plus correct paddling techniques. Come out and try this fun and exciting way to enjoy our beautiful water ways. Paddle board, paddle and PFD will be provided.

Registration

Registration is required the Monday before each session. For quickest registration or to check availability of a program, please visit our online registration center. You may also email our scheduling department at scheduling@chattnaturecenter.org or call 770-992-2055 x237.
