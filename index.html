Walk on Water

Who We Are

Walk on Water was established in May of 2011 by Brian Burdett, who brought stand up paddle boarding instruction to Roswell and the Chattahoochee River. WoW, in partnership with the City of Roswell Parks and Recreation, is dedicated to sharing their paddling knowledge with those that want to learn more about this incredible sport. Let us help you explore the Chattahoochee River in a brand new way!

WoW's partnership with the Chattahoochee Nature Center focuses on learning the basics of stand up paddle boarding while exploring their serene pond.

Like us on Facebook and interact with fellow paddleboarders! Be sure to post comments and your latest paddle boarding adventure.

Come paddle down the Chattahoochee with us!

Roswell Parks & Recreation

Welcome to Roswell

From the hundreds of programs offered to residents to the City’s beautiful trail system, Roswell’s recreation and parks services are second to none in the State of Georgia. Roswell has 890.38 acres of parkland, or 10.5 acres per 1,000 residents, which exceeds the national recommendation of 7 acres per 1,000 residents. Roswell Recreation & Parks Department is nationally accredited and has been recognized an unprecedented eight times by the Georgia Parks & Recreation Association for having the best department in the state.

Read more about Recreation, Parks, Historic and Cultural Affairs at www.roswellgov.com.

Paddle101

This 4 week course focuses on the fundamentals of paddle and stroke techniques, board control, and navigation. The course will also go into the finer aspects of paddle boarding, including theory, self-launching, self-rescue, posture, balance, endurance, equipment advice, basic water rescue, critical decision making, and etiquette, with emphasis on safety and weather assessment. Paddle board, paddle and PFD will be provided.

Chattahoochee Nature Center

Explore the Chattahoochee

The Chattahoochee Nature Center is located on the Chattahoochee River in Roswell. It sits on a beautiful site comprised of 127 acres of native plants and gardens that showcase the beauty of Georgia. The CNC has a River Boardwalk, Discovery Center, wetland demonstration gardens and woodland trails that are home to over 50 species of injured, non-releasable wildlife. For 30 years, this facility has continued to grow and reach out to citizens as a place to explore new ideas and expand the awareness of the natural world.

Stand Up Paddle Boarding Pond Exploration

Come take a walk on the mild side at the Chattahoochee Nature Center with a paddle board class on a Georgia Wetlands pond. We help those new to paddle boarding (and those who just want to practice) learn the basics of stand up paddle boarding. This class will teach the fundamentals of SUP without dealing with river current. Come out and try this fun and exciting way to enjoy Chattahoochee Nature Center! This class is suited for all ages 8 and up. Paddle board, paddle and PFD will be provided.

Our Instructor

Brian Burdett

The coolest paddleboarder this side of the Chattahoochee!
