Rentals & Classes

Instructor Lead SUP Lessons

We offer a fleet of BIC Standup Paddle Boards through Shoot the Hooch at Roswell's Azalea Park, located at 203 Azalea Drive, Roswell, Georgia.

In addition to standup paddle boards, Shoot the Hooch is the premier rental outfitters right on the Chattahoochee River offering raft, canoe, kayak, tube rentals.

Stand up paddle boarding (SUP) is a great way to get out and enjoy the gentle current of the Chattahoochee River. Open to all experience levels, SUP is an amazing full-body workout that is quickly growing in popularity.

We offer hourly and full day paddle board rentals from our Azalea Park location 7 days/week June through August and Weekends during May, September, and October. We also offer a variety of lessons, including a SUP 101 class for beginners and a SUP yoga class for those looking for a fun and relaxing way to get a workout.

SUP School

Starting out with our beginners SUP class gives you a solid foundation of all the basics to paddle correctly and safely. This one hour class is designed to get ages 11-70+ out on the water. Wide, stable boards are provided to get you standing and paddling the river in no time at all.

Note: To receive a refund on a cancellation, we must be notified 14 days prior to the class reservation date, otherwise you will be given a raincheck. If your trip is cancelled or cut short due to weather or river conditions, you will be given a rain-check for a later date.
